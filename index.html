Context & Law

The Invisible Dome

To understand drone journalism around Disney parks, one must first understand the legal anomaly that protects them. Unlike other private properties, Disney parks are shielded by a federal "No Fly Zone."

Following 9/11, Congress passed a spending bill that included a permanent Temporary Flight Restriction (TFR) over Walt Disney World and Disneyland. This 3-mile radius restriction prohibits aircraft (including drones) from flying below 3,000 feet. This creates a unique challenge for journalists and hobbyists attempting to capture aerial footage of park construction or operations.
